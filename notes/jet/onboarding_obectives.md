# Onboarding Objectives: Understanding of Reliability - PI process - due 30.06.2026
 
## Develop Service-Level Objectives (SLOs)

Know the SLOs for at least one key platform service, including metrics for availability, latency, and error rate.
SLOs provide a target for reliability and are key to tracking progress.

## Enhance Node Autoscaling Efficiency

Optimize Kubernetes cluster autoscaling configurations, reducing response times to workload spikes by 30%, ensuring reliable resource availability.
Autoscaling is vital for maintaining reliability during traffic changes.

## Understand how to implement proactive resource request limits

Define and enforce resource request and limit policies for at least 80% of all pods in EKS to prevent resource starvation and improve service reliability, since proper resource management directly impacts service reliability.

## Understand how Post-mortem are carried out

Hopefully participate in a post-mortem to understand the process and how to most effectively learn from PIs.
