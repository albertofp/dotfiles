# Kubeflow introduction

## What is Kubeflow?
- ML toolkit for Kubernetes
    - data preparation
    - model training
    - prediction serving
    - service management

## Goals
- Easy, repeatable, portable deployments
- Loosely coupled microservices
- Scaling based on demand
