1. Leave the Door Open: 6/10 - Paak is cool, I like the instrumentals, lyrics are meh, video is chill and kinda funny? Mars is ok but the chorus is the weakest part of the song tbh, I feel like he tries too hard here and it doesnt fit? Never a fan of "la la la" sections lol

2. Fly As Me: 7.5/10 - I like the James Brown vibes (and sample at the start?) Kind of over the top sillyness, funny in a good way.  Mars does much better here, less "over the top singing"

3. After Last Night w/ Thundercat & Bootsy: 4/10 - Not my kind of song tbh, a lot of the horny/romantic and dramatic singing. The ad libs don't work here IMO, kinda distracting and don't add anything. Feats are good, they complement Paak and Mars very well. Electric guitar is a cool touch but too little of it

4. Smokin Out The Window: 7/10 - Paak is funny and I can tolerate the dancing and melodramatics.  Chorus is cool, and this is the best Mars song out of the "I dont like Bruno Mars" songs for me tbh and he really means the "This Bitch"

5. Love Train: 3.5/10 - Unexpected Chapelle lmao and good intro, video is very silly.  Song was a disappointment compared to the video ngl, feels like nothing really happens and it's just very bland. I just realized this is probably not the official music video but it enhanced the experience and the song would have gotten a worse rating without it

6. Put On A Smile: 3.5 - Same issues as before but cool drums and no cool video.  I like drums so this makes up for it.  Mars needs to chill out

7. 777: 7/10 - This is more unique and more so my thing.  Again a lot of not so subtle James Brown influence which is a plus, and some rock?  Not quite Fly As Me level but it's cool.  GTA radio kinda song maybe

8. Skate: 4/10 - Feels like "Leave the Door Open 2.0" but worse, meh. Video Has more going on but it's a negative here.  Hairdresser waiting room song I would be able to just tune out, not unlike "Put On A Smile" but this has a least a little bit of personality I guess, maybe not

9. Blast Off: 6/10 - I like the instrumentals, but again I wish there was less Bruno Mars in my Anderson Paak song.  Big fan of the guitars, reminds me of Santana a little.  Should have leaned more on the instrumentals IMO. Not a terrible song but weak finish compared to the good start to the album.  This is like the cold curry leftovers I had today, still good and I see where there was more but meh
